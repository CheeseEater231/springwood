Welcome to the New South Wales Police Force Internal Portal

This portal provides authorised personnel with access to executive information, divisions, procedures, and operational systems.

Executive Leadership Team

Commissioner of Police

Responsible for overall command, strategic direction, and leadership of the NSW Police Force.

Deputy Commissioner

Supports the Commissioner and oversees daily operations across all commands and divisions.

Assistant Commissioner - Metropolitan Field Operations

Manages metropolitan policing, patrols, and operational resources across Sydney and surrounding areas.

Assistant Commissioner - Investigations & Counter Terrorism

Leads criminal investigations, intelligence gathering, and counter-terrorism initiatives across the state.

Assistant Commissioner - People & Capability Command

Oversees workforce management, training, recruitment, and professional development for all NSW Police personnel.

Divisions and Their Functions

General Duties

Frontline police services, community engagement, and day-to-day law enforcement activities across all regions.

Highway Patrol

Ensures safety on major roads, enforces traffic laws, and responds to road incidents across New South Wales.

State Crime Command

Investigates serious and organised crime, provides specialist investigative support to regional and metropolitan areas.

Public Order and Riot Squad

Specialised response unit for crowd control, riots, protests, and high-risk public order events.

Tactical Operations Unit

Handles critical incidents, armed offenders, hostage situations, and high-risk operations.

Professional Standards Command

Monitors compliance, investigates complaints against police, and ensures integrity and accountability within the force.

Education and Training Command

Delivers police training programs, professional development, and continuous learning initiatives for officers.
