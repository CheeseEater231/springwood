Welcome to the New South Wales Police Force

Serving the community with integrity, professionalism, and commitment to public safety across New South Wales.

Executive Leadership Team

Commissioner of Police

Responsible for the overall leadership and strategic direction of the NSW Police Force.

Deputy Commissioner

Oversees day-to-day operations and supports the Commissioner in all strategic matters.

Assistant Commissioner - Metropolitan Field Operations

Manages policing operations across metropolitan Sydney, including frontline patrols and community safety.

Assistant Commissioner - Investigations & Counter Terrorism

Leads criminal investigations, counter-terrorism initiatives, and intelligence operations.

Assistant Commissioner - People & Capability Command

Responsible for recruitment, training, workforce management, and professional development.

Divisions and Their Functions

General Duties

Frontline police providing community safety, crime prevention, and everyday law enforcement across NSW.

Highway Patrol

Responsible for road safety, traffic law enforcement, and responding to incidents on major highways and roads.

State Crime Command

Investigates serious, organised, and complex crimes with specialist teams and resources.

Public Order and Riot Squad

Specialised unit for managing large-scale public order events, protests, and riots.

Tactical Operations Unit

Responds to critical incidents including armed offenders, hostage situations, and high-risk operations.

Professional Standards Command

Ensures integrity, accountability, and public confidence through investigations of complaints and misconduct.

Education and Training Command

Delivers police education, professional development, and continuous training programs for officers.
